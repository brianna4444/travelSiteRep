About Me

Owner/ Lead Agent

I have been a travel agent since 2010. My experience started young with my parents traveling in the US. It wasn't long before I was falling in love exploring new places. I continued my passion as an adult with my own family. Road trips and Disney are still favorites of mine. We soon began cruising and staying at all inclusive resorts in Mexico and the Caribbean. Taking excursions to learn new activities or cultural tours are high on my list. Visiting Mayan villages, ziplining in a Costa Rican rainforest or touring an antebellum home are a few examples of my diversity.

I enjoy learning and can assist with most destinations or types of travel. We have access to flights around the world to check off those items on your bucket list or just enjoy some relaxation on the beach. If you don't like to fly, there are cross country trips by rail or bus. For cruisers, there are sailings close enough to drive or many itineraries are available on rivers in the US. My clients have different interests and activity levels so I have a wide range of knowledge. Let me find the perfect trip for your next trip!
